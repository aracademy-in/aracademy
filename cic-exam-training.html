CIC Course

Certified Inpatient Coder (CIC) credential is dedicated exclusively to inpatient hospital/facility coding, validating expert-level knowledge in ICD-10-CM/PCS coding and specialized payment knowledge in MS-DRGs and IPPS.

Investing in the CIC credential can lead to a 40% higher earning potential for certified coders compared to non-credentialed coders.

CIC Exam Pattern & Books

  • ICD-10-CM code book (Current year), any publisher


  • ICD-10-PCS code book (Current year), any publisher.


  • 40 multiple-choice questions and 7 inpatient cases fill-in-the-blank (proctored) question


  • 4 hours to complete the exam - Open Code Books (manuals)


  • 70% passing score required

Objectives of CIC Training

Master inpatient medical coding with AR Academy's comprehensive CIC training program

Gain expertise in ICD-10 coding for diagnoses and procedures in inpatient settings

Proficiency in reviewing medical records to abstract essential information for accurate inpatient coding.

Expertise in assigning precise ICD-10 codes for diagnoses and procedures in the inpatient setting.

Gain a comprehensive understanding of the latest regulations and issues in medical coding, compliance, and reimbursement, specifically focusing on MS-DRG and IPPS systems.

Strong ability to adapt to coding and reimbursement rule changes, including updating the CDM, fees, and UB04 Field Locators.

Solid understanding of anatomy, physiology, and medical terminology, vital for correct coding of facility services and diagnoses.

In-depth knowledge of different hospital types and departments, including teaching hospitals, ASCs, and outpatient departments.

Mastery in the business aspects of hospital inpatient services, differentiating between inpatient and outpatient services, payment methodologies, and compliance regulations.

FAQ

Can I take CIC training online?
Yes, many institutions offer online CIC training programs. Online training provides flexibility and allows you to study at your own pace.
What are the Eligibility for CIC training?
There are no specific prerequisites for CIC training. However, having a background in medical coding or healthcare can be beneficial.
How is the CIC exam Pattern?
The CIC exam consists of 40 multiple-choice questions and 7 inpatient case fill-in-the-blank questions. Candidates have 4 hours to complete the exam, and a passing score of 70% is required.
What resources are allowed during the CIC exam?
During the CIC exam, candidates are allowed to use open code books or manuals.
How can CIC training benefit my career?
CIC training enhances your coding skills and demonstrates your expertise in inpatient coding. It can open up new career opportunities in hospitals, medical facilities, and healthcare organizations.
What is CIC salary package in india and abroad?
CIC salary in India ranges from INR 2.5-15 lakhs per year, depending on experience. Abroad, CIC salaries vary, with entry-level around $40k-$60k and experienced professionals earning $60k-$100k+ annually
What is CIC exam fees ?
Exam fees is 775 dollar
What is the maximum number of attempts for the CIC exam per payment?
The maximum number of attempts for the CIC exam per payment is two.
